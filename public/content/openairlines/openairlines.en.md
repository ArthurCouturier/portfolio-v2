## Helping airlines save fuel ✈️

OpenAirlines is a fuel-efficiency company that helps airlines save fuel. I worked there as a freelance Data Engineer across **three successive contracts**, developing and maintaining the data-integration pipeline at the heart of the product.

## The data-integration pipeline 🔗

I developed and maintained the pipeline that ingests, processes and serves flight data. Day to day, I optimized data-processing algorithms and resolved production incidents through in-depth SQL analysis — digging into the data to find the root cause rather than patching symptoms.

## An international, agile context 🌍

I was a member of the **Off Cycle** team, working in a Shape Up agile context. I collaborated daily with international teams (mostly in Asia and the Americas) to fix client-reported bugs and ship new custom-made features — coordinating across time zones and turning real customer feedback into concrete improvements.
