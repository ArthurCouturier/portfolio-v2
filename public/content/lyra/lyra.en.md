## Payment systems at scale 💳

Lyra Network is a payment service provider. I joined its **e-commerce department** as a back-end engineer (fixed-term contract), contributing to the systems that process online payments for a large base of merchants and customers.

## Migrating from JBoss to Spring Boot 🚀

I led the migration of payment solutions from **JBoss to Spring Boot**, ensuring a smooth transition and improved overall system performance. The key constraint: the change had to be completely transparent for every customer using the payment method I worked on — no disruption, no regression on a system handling real money.

## Modernizing the codebase 🧰

Beyond the migration, I contributed to a broader codebase-modernization effort, working in a Scrum agile context alongside the rest of the team.
